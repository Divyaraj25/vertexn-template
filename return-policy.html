Return & Refund Policy

Home / Return Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Our Return Promise

At Vertexn, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We offer a 15-day return policy on most products, giving you peace of mind with every purchase.

Hassle-Free Returns

If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help!

2. Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

Unused condition
Original tags attached
Original packaging
Proof of purchase
Within 15 days of delivery

3. How to Initiate a Return

1

Contact Us

Email support@vertexn.com with your order number and reason for return

2

Get Approval

We'll review your request and provide return instructions within 24-48 hours

3

Ship the Item

Pack the item securely and ship to the provided return address

4

Inspection & Refund

We'll inspect the return and process your refund within 5-7 business days

4. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

Sale Items

Clearance and discounted products

Gift Cards

Non-refundable

Personal Care

Due to hygiene reasons

Custom Products

Personalized or custom-made items

Perishable Goods

Food items, flowers, etc.

Opened Items

Items with broken seals

5. Refund Process

Payment Method Refund Timeline Notes
Credit/Debit Card 5-7 business days Depends on bank processing
UPI / Net Banking 3-5 business days Usually faster
Wallets 24-48 hours Instant wallet credit
Cash on Delivery 7-10 business days Bank transfer required

Once refund is processed, you'll receive a confirmation email. Please note that your bank may take additional time to reflect the amount in your account.

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Report within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage/defect
  • Keep the original packaging for inspection
  • We'll arrange for a free replacement or full refund

7. Wrong Item Received

If you received the wrong item:

  1. Contact us immediately at support@vertexn.com
  2. Provide your order number and photos of the received item
  3. We'll arrange pickup of the wrong item
  4. Correct item will be shipped with priority

8. Exchanges

For exchanges (size, color, etc.):

  • Return the original item following the return process
  • Place a new order for the desired item
  • This ensures fastest processing of your exchange

9. Return Shipping

  • Defective/Damaged/Wrong Items: Free return pickup arranged by us
  • Change of Mind Returns: Customer bears return shipping costs
  • Shipping charges: Original shipping charges are non-refundable
  • Return address: Provided after return approval

10. Cancellation Policy

  • Within 2 hours of order: Free cancellation
  • After 2 hours: Order processing may have started, cancellation subject to approval
  • Shipped orders: Cannot be cancelled, must follow return process
