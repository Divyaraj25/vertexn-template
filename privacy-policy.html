Your Privacy Matters to Us

At Vertexn, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use our services, we may collect:

Identity Information

Name, date of birth, gender

Contact Information

Email address, phone number

Address Information

Shipping address, billing address

Payment Information

Card details (processed by Razorpay)

Order Information

Purchase history, preferences

User Content

Reviews, photos, feedback

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our site, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, navigation path
  • Location Data: General geographic location
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device

We use your information for:

Order Processing

To process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and returns

Customer Service

To respond to your inquiries and provide support

Improvement

To analyze and improve our website and services

Marketing

To send promotional offers (with your consent)

Fraud Prevention

To detect and prevent fraudulent transactions

Legal Compliance

To comply with legal obligations

We share your information only with trusted third parties necessary to provide our services:

Payment Processors

Razorpay - for secure payment processing

Analytics Providers

Google Analytics - to understand site usage

Email Service Providers

For order updates and marketing (with consent)

Important: We NEVER sell your personal information to third parties.

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL Encryption: 256-bit encryption for all data transmission
