Frequently Asked Questions

Is this dishwasher safe?

We recommend hand washing to maintain the quality of the glass and the pourer. The heat of the dishwasher may affect the stainless steel components over time.

Can I use this for hot oil?

Yes, the borosilicate glass is heat resistant and can handle hot oil up to 150°C. However, we recommend letting the oil cool slightly before pouring.

Does it come with a warranty?

Yes, we offer a 6-month warranty against manufacturing defects. Please contact our support team if you face any issues.

Is the pourer leak-proof?

Yes, the stainless steel pourer has an airtight seal that prevents leaking. The bottle also comes with a cap for added protection when not in use.
