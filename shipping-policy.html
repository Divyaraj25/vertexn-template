1. Order Processing

At Vertexn, we strive to process and ship your orders as quickly as possible.

  • Processing Time: 1-2 business days (Monday-Friday, excluding holidays)
  • Cut-off Time: Orders placed before 2 PM IST are processed the same day
  • Order Verification: High-value orders may require phone verification
  • Weekend Orders: Orders placed on Saturday-Sunday are processed on Monday

5. Shipping Charges

  • Free Shipping: On all orders above ₹499
  • Standard Shipping: ₹49 - ₹99 (based on weight and destination)
  • Express Shipping: ₹149 - ₹199 (based on weight and destination)

  • Cash on Delivery: ₹40 additional fee for COD orders

8. Undeliverable Orders

If a package is returned as undeliverable:

  • Wrong Address: Customer is responsible for correct address. Re-shipping charges apply for re-delivery.
  • Unclaimed Package: If not collected from courier office, refund will be issued minus shipping costs.
  • Refused Delivery: Refund will be processed minus both-way shipping charges.

9. Lost or Damaged Packages

  • Lost Packages: File a claim with us if not delivered within 15 days of shipping. We'll investigate and process replacement/refund.
  • Damaged Packages: Report within 48 hours of delivery with photos. We'll arrange replacement or refund.
  • Delayed Packages: Contact us if delivery exceeds estimated time by 5+ business days.

10. Shipping Restrictions

Some products may have shipping restrictions:

  • No shipping to PO Box addresses
  • Some remote areas may have extended delivery times
  • Certain products cannot be shipped to specific regions due to courier restrictions
