Frequently Asked Questions

Home / FAQ

How can we help you?

Orders

How do I place an order?

Placing an order is easy! Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your shipping details, choose a payment method, and confirm your order. You'll receive an order confirmation email within minutes.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it. After that, orders are processed for shipping and cannot be changed. Contact our support team immediately at support@vertexn.com for assistance.

How do I know if my order is confirmed?

After successfully placing your order, you'll receive an order confirmation email with your order details and order number. You can also check your order status in your account dashboard.

What is your order processing time?

Orders are typically processed within 24-48 hours. During peak seasons or sales, processing may take slightly longer. You'll receive a shipping confirmation once your order is dispatched.

Shipping

What are your shipping charges?

Shipping charges vary based on order value and location:

  • Free shipping on orders above ₹499
  • Standard shipping: ₹49 - ₹99 (3-7 business days)
  • Express shipping: ₹149 - ₹199 (2-4 business days)

How long does shipping take?

Delivery times vary by location:

  • Metro cities: 2-4 business days
  • Tier 2-3 cities: 4-7 business days
  • Remote locations: 5-8 business days

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order on our Track Order page or directly on the courier partner's website.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding to international shipping soon. Subscribe to our newsletter for updates!

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 15-day return policy. Items must be unused, with original tags and packaging. To initiate a return, contact us at support@vertexn.com with your order number and reason for return.

How long do refunds take?

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The amount will be credited to your original payment method. Your bank may take additional 2-3 days to reflect the amount.

Can I exchange a product?

For exchanges, please return the original item and place a new order. This ensures you get the correct item fastest. Contact our support team for assistance.

What items are non-returnable?

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Sale items and clearance products
  • Gift cards
  • Personal care items (due to hygiene)
  • Custom or personalized products

Products

Are your products authentic?

Yes! We source directly from manufacturers and authorized distributors. All products are 100% authentic and come with manufacturer warranty where applicable.

Do you provide product warranties?

Most of our products come with a manufacturer warranty ranging from 3 months to 1 year. Check individual product pages for warranty information.

How do I care for my products?

Care instructions vary by product. Please refer to the product description or packaging for specific care guidelines. For most kitchen items, hand washing is recommended.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, Amex)
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM)
  • Net Banking
  • Wallets (Paytm, Amazon Pay)
  • Cash on Delivery (COD)

Is it safe to use my credit card?

Absolutely! We use Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. All transactions are encrypted and secure. We never store your card details.

Do you offer cash on delivery?

Yes, Cash on Delivery is available for orders under ₹5,000. A nominal fee of ₹40 may apply for COD orders.

Account

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Account" icon in the header and select "Register". Fill in your details to create an account. You can also create an account during checkout.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password.

How do I update my account information?

Log in to your account and go to "Account Settings". You can update your personal information, address, and preferences there.
